2025

Hotel Afisha Opens in the Heart of Tbilisi

A new five-star design hotel brings Georgian cinema heritage to life through theatre aesthetic blended with retro-futuristic elements. Created by designer Esma Khetsuriani, the property features a Brasserie, Wine Room, and rooftop Bubble Bar on the 8th floor.

2025

Where Cinema Meets Hospitality

Hotel Afisha combines European ambiance with a distinctive conceptual approach, appealing to guests interested in cultural history, conceptual design, and the fusion of historical and contemporary elements in interior, cuisine, and hospitality.

2025

Bubble Bar — Tbilisi's Newest Rooftop Destination

Located on the 8th floor, Bubble Bar offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city skyline with a vibrant disco atmosphere, quickly becoming one of Tbilisi's most sought-after evening destinations.
